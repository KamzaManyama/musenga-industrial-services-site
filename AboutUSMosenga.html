Who We Are

Proudly South African

Musenga Investments (Pty) Ltd is more than just a service provider we’re a purpose-driven enterprise born from the vision of creating impact where it matters most. Established in 2017 and proudly 100% Black woman-owned, our roots trace back to Musengavhadzimu Business Enterprise cc (founded in 2002), with a deep commitment to excellence in every solution we offer.

Based in Midrand, Gauteng, we offer integrated services in waste management, construction, and commercial & industrial cleaning. Our reach spans across multiple provinces, working with both public and private sectors to deliver efficient, sustainable, and community-focused solutions.

At our core, we believe transformation starts with opportunity. That’s why we actively create employment, upskill local communities, and partner with organizations that share our passion for empowerment and progress.
